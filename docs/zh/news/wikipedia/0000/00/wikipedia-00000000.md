## 阿尔弗雷德_希区柯克_电影记录   
Alfred Hitchcock (1899–1980) was an English director and filmmaker. Popularly known as the "Master of Suspense" for his use of innovative film techniques in thrillers, Hitchcock started his career in the British film industry as a title designer and art director for a number of silent films during the early 1920s. His directorial debut was the 1925 release The Pleasure Garden. Hitchcock followed this with The Lodger: A Story of the London Fog, his first commercial and critical success. It featured many of the thematic elements his films would be known for, such as an innocent man on the run. It also featured the first of his famous cameo appearances. Two years later he directed Blackmail (1929) which was his first sound film. In 1935, Hitchcock directed The 39 Steps; three years later, he directed The Lady Vanishes, starring Margaret Lockwood and Michael Redgrave.   

阿尔弗雷德·希区柯克（Alfred Hitchcock，1899-1980）是一位英国导演和电影制片人。希区柯克因在惊悚片中运用创新的电影技术而被誉为“悬疑大师”，他在英国电影界开始了自己的职业生涯，担任20世纪20年代初多部无声电影的标题设计师和艺术总监。他的导演处女作是1925年上映的《欢乐花园》。希区柯克紧随其后，出版了《房客：伦敦雾的故事》，这是他的第一部商业和评论界的成功。它包含了他的电影因其闻名的许多主题元素，例如一个逃亡的无辜者。它还展示了他第一次著名的客串。两年后，他执导了他的第一部有声电影《勒索》（Blackmail，1929）。1935年，希区柯克执导了《39级台阶》;三年后，他执导了《消失的女士》，由玛格丽特·洛克伍德和迈克尔·雷德格雷夫主演。

## Shope_乳头状瘤_病毒   
The Shope papilloma virus (SPV), also known as cottontail rabbit papilloma virus (CRPV) or Kappapapillomavirus 2, is a papillomavirus which infects certain leporids, causing keratinous carcinomas resembling horns, typically on or near the animal's head. The carcinomas can metastasize or become large enough to interfere with the host's ability to eat, causing starvation. Richard E. Shope investigated the horns and discovered the virus in 1933, an important breakthrough in the study of oncoviruses. The virus was originally discovered in cottontail rabbits in the Midwestern U.S. but can also infect brush rabbits, black-tailed jackrabbits, snowshoe hares, European rabbits, and domestic rabbits.   

肖普乳头瘤病毒（SPV），也称为棉尾兔乳头瘤病毒（CRPV）或卡帕帕瘤病毒2，是一种乳头瘤病毒，感染某些野兔，引起类似角的角化癌，通常在动物头部上或附近。癌细胞可能会转移或变得足够大，干扰宿主的进食能力，导致饥饿。Richard E.肖普于1933年研究了角并发现了病毒，这是癌病毒研究的重要突破。该病毒最初在美国中西部的棉尾兔中发现，但也可以感染灌木兔、黑尾大野兔、雪鞋野兔、欧洲兔和家兔。

## 赛亚拉   
Saiyaara is a 2025 Indian Hindi-language musical romantic drama film directed by Mohit Suri. Produced under Yash Raj Films, it stars debutant Ahaan Panday and Aneet Padda. It is loosely based on the 2004 Korean film A Moment To Remember. The film follows Krish Kapoor, a troubled musician, as he forms a deep connection with Vaani Batra, a shy poet.   

《Saiyaara》是一部2025年印度印地语音乐浪漫剧情片，由莫希特·苏瑞执导。该片由Yash Raj Films制作，由首次亮相的阿汉·潘迪（Ahaan Panday）和阿尼特·帕达（Aneet Padda）主演。它大致改编自2004年韩国电影《难忘的时刻》。这部电影讲述了陷入困境的音乐家克里什·卡普尔与害羞诗人瓦尼·巴特拉建立了深厚的联系。
