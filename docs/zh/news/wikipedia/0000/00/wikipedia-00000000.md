## 格陵兰   
Greenland is a North American island autonomous territory of the Kingdom of Denmark. It is the larger of two autonomous territories within the Kingdom, the other being the Faroe Islands; the citizens of both territories are full citizens of Denmark. As Greenland is one of the Overseas Countries and Territories of the European Union, citizens of Greenland are European Union citizens. The capital and largest city of Greenland is Nuuk. 格陵兰岛是丹麦王国的北美岛屿自治领土。它是王国内两个自治领土中较大的一个，另一个是法罗群岛;这两个领土的公民都是丹麦的正式公民。由于格陵兰岛是欧盟海外国家和领土之一，格陵兰岛公民也是欧盟公民。格陵兰岛的首府和最大城市是努克。格陵兰岛位于北冰洋和大西洋之间，加拿大北极群岛以东。它是世界上最大的岛屿，也是世界上最北端的陆地所在地-北海岸的卡费克卢本岛是世界上最北端无可争议的陆地，而大陆上的莫里斯角杰苏普一直被认为是这样，直到20世纪60年代。

## 奥德赛   
The Odyssey is one of two major ancient Greek epic poems attributed to Homer. It is one of the oldest works of literature still widely read by modern audiences. Like the Iliad, the Odyssey is divided into 24 books. It follows the Greek hero Odysseus, king of Ithaca, and his journey home after the Trojan War. After the war, which lasted ten years, his journey from Troy to Ithaca, via Africa and southern Europe, lasted for ten additional years during which time he encountered many perils and all of his crewmates were killed. In his absence, Odysseus was assumed dead, and his wife Penelope and son Telemachus had to contend with a group of unruly suitors who were competing for Penelope's hand in marriage.   

《奥德赛》是荷马创作的两首主要古希腊史诗之一。这是至今仍被现代观众广泛阅读的最古老的文学作品之一。与《伊利亚特》一样，《奥德赛》分为24本书。它讲述了希腊英雄、伊萨卡国王奥德修斯在特洛伊战争后回家的经历。持续十年的战争结束后，他从特洛伊经非洲和南欧前往伊萨卡的旅程又持续了十年，在此期间他遇到了许多危险，所有的船员都被杀了。在他缺席的情况下，奥德修斯被认为已经死亡，他的妻子佩内洛普和儿子忒勒马科斯不得不与一群不守规矩的追求者竞争，他们正在争夺佩内洛普的婚姻。

## 圣诞   
Christmas is an annual festival commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ, observed primarily on December 25 as a religious and cultural celebration among billions of people around the world. A liturgical feast central to Christianity, preparation for Christmas begins on the First Sunday of Advent and it is followed by Christmastide, which historically in the West lasts twelve days and culminates on Twelfth Night. Christmas Day is a public holiday in many countries, is observed religiously by a majority of Christians, as well as celebrated culturally by many non-Christians, and forms an integral part of the annual holiday season.   

圣诞节是纪念耶稣基督诞生的一年一度的节日，主要在12月25日庆祝，作为世界各地数十亿人的宗教和文化庆祝活动。圣诞节是基督教的核心礼拜盛宴，圣诞节的准备工作从降临节的第一个星期日开始，随后是圣诞节，历史上，在西方，圣诞节持续十二天，在第十二夜达到高潮。圣诞节在许多国家都是公共假日，大多数基督徒都以宗教方式庆祝，许多非基督徒也在文化上庆祝，并且是一年一度假期不可或缺的一部分。

## 斯嘉丽_约翰逊   
Scarlett Ingrid Johansson is an American actress and singer. The world's highest-paid actress in 2018 and 2019, she has been featured multiple times on the Forbes Celebrity 100 list. Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2021. Johansson's films have grossed over $15.4 billion worldwide, making her the highest-grossing box office female star of all time. She has received various accolades, including a British Academy Film Award, a Tony Award, and nominations for two Academy Awards and five Golden Globe Awards.   

斯嘉丽·英格丽德·约翰逊是一位美国女演员和歌手。她是2018年和2019年全球收入最高的女演员，多次入选福布斯名人100强榜单。《时代》杂志将她评为2021年全球最具影响力的100位人物之一。约翰逊的电影在全球票房收入超过154亿美元，使她成为有史以来票房最高的女明星。她获得了各种荣誉，包括英国电影学院奖、托尼奖以及两项奥斯卡奖和五项金球奖的提名。
