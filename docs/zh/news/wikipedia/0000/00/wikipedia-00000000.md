## 巴拿马_运河   
The Panama Canal is an artificial 82-kilometer (51-mile) waterway in Panama that connects the Atlantic Ocean with the Pacific Ocean, cutting across the Isthmus of Panama, and is a conduit for maritime trade. Locks at each end lift ships up to Gatun Lake, an artificial fresh water lake 26 meters (85 ft) above sea level, created by damming up the Chagres River and Lake Alajuela to reduce the amount of excavation work required for the canal. Locks then lower the ships at the other end. An average of 200,000,000 L (52,000,000 US gal) of fresh water is used in a single passing of a ship. The canal is threatened by low water levels during droughts.   

巴拿马运河是巴拿马一条长82公里（51英里）的人工水道，连接大西洋和太平洋，穿过巴拿马地峡，是海上贸易的管道。两端的锁将船只提升至加通湖，这是一个海拔26米（85英尺）的人工淡水湖，是通过堵住查格雷斯河和阿拉胡埃拉湖而形成的，以减少运河所需的挖掘工作量。然后，锁将另一端的船只放下。一艘船一次通过平均使用200，000，000升（52，00，000美国加仑）的淡水。干旱期间，运河受到低水位的威胁。

## 格陵兰   
Greenland is a North American island autonomous territory of the Kingdom of Denmark. It is the larger of two autonomous territories within the Kingdom, the other being the Faroe Islands; the citizens of both territories are full citizens of Denmark. As Greenland is one of the Overseas Countries and Territories of the European Union, citizens of Greenland are European Union citizens. The capital and largest city of Greenland is Nuuk. Greenland lies between the Arctic and Atlantic oceans, east of the Canadian Arctic Archipelago. It is the world's largest island, and is the location of the northernmost point of land in the world – Kaffeklubben Island off the northern coast is the world's northernmost undisputed point of land, and Cape Morris Jesup on the mainland was thought to be so until the 1960s.   

格陵兰岛是丹麦王国的北美岛屿自治领土。它是王国内两个自治领土中较大的一个，另一个是法罗群岛;这两个领土的公民都是丹麦的正式公民。由于格陵兰岛是欧盟海外国家和领土之一，格陵兰岛公民也是欧盟公民。格陵兰岛的首府和最大城市是努克。格陵兰岛位于北冰洋和大西洋之间，加拿大北极群岛以东。它是世界上最大的岛屿，也是世界上最北端的陆地所在地-北海岸的卡费克卢本岛是世界上最北端无可争议的陆地，而大陆上的莫里斯角杰苏普一直被认为是这样，直到20世纪60年代。

## Colin_Jost   
Colin Kelly Jost is an American comedian, writer, and actor. Jost has been a staff writer for the NBC sketch comedy series Saturday Night Live since 2005, and co-anchor of Weekend Update since 2014. He also served as one of the show's co-head writers from 2012 to 2015 and later came back as one of the show's head writers in 2017 until 2022, alongside Michael Che.   

科林·凯利·约斯特是一位美国喜剧演员、作家和演员。Jost自2005年以来一直担任NBC喜剧小品剧《周六夜现场》的特约撰稿人，并自2014年以来担任周末更新的联合主持人。他还于2012年至2015年担任该剧的联合首席编剧之一，后来于2017年至2022年与Michael Che一起重新担任该剧的首席编剧之一。
