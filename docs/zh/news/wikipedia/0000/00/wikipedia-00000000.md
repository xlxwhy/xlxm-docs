## 瑞奇_亨德森   
Rickey Nelson Henley Henderson, nicknamed Man of Steal, was an American professional baseball left fielder who played 24 seasons in Major League Baseball (MLB) for nine teams from 1979 to 2003, including four separate tenures with his original team, the Oakland Athletics. He is widely regarded as baseball's greatest leadoff hitter and baserunner. He holds MLB records for career stolen bases, runs, unintentional walks, and leadoff home runs. At the time of his last major league game in 2003, the ten-time American League (AL) All-Star ranked among the sport's top 100 all-time home run hitters and was its all-time leader in walks. In 2009, he was inducted to the Baseball Hall of Fame in his first year of eligibility.   

里基·尼尔森·亨利·亨德森（Rickey Nelson Henley Henderson），绰号“偷来的人”，是一名美国职业棒球左外野手，从1979年到2003年，他在美国职业棒球大联盟（MLB）为9支球队效力了24个赛季，其中包括在他原来的球队奥克兰运动家队担任四个单独的任期。他被广泛认为是棒球界最伟大的领先击球手和垒手。他保持着职业生涯偷垒、跑动、无意散步和领先本垒打的MLB记录。2003年，这位十届美国联盟（AL）全明星球员在他参加最后一场大联盟比赛时，跻身该运动历史上前100名全垒打手之列，并且是该运动历史上的领跑者。2009年，他入选棒球名人堂的第一年。

## 冬至   
The winter solstice, or hibernal solstice, occurs when either of Earth's poles reaches its maximum tilt away from the Sun. This happens twice yearly, once in each hemisphere. For that hemisphere, the winter solstice is the day with the shortest period of daylight and longest night of the year, and when the Sun is at its lowest daily maximum elevation in the sky. Each polar region experiences continuous darkness or twilight around its winter solstice. The opposite event is the summer solstice.   

冬至或冬眠至发生在地球的任何一个极点达到远离太阳的最大倾斜度时。这种情况每年发生两次，每个半球一次。对于该半球来说，冬至是一年中日照时间最短、夜晚最长的一天，也是太阳处于天空中每日最低高度的一天。每个极地地区在冬至前后都会经历持续的黑暗或黄昏。相反的事件是夏至。
