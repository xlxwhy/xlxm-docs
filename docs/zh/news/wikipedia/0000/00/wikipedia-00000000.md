## 蒙蒂霍尔问题   
The Monty Hall problem is a brain teaser, in the form of a probability puzzle, based nominally on the American television game show Let's Make a Deal and named after its original host, Monty Hall. The problem was originally posed in a letter by Steve Selvin to the American Statistician in 1975. It became famous as a question from reader Craig F. Whitaker's letter quoted in Marilyn vos Savant's "Ask Marilyn" column in Parade magazine in 1990:Suppose you're on a game show, and you're given the choice of three doors: Behind one door is a car; behind the others, goats. You pick a door, say No. 1, and the host, who knows what's behind the doors, opens another door, say No. 3, which has a goat. He then says to you, "Do you want to pick door No. 2?" Is it to your advantage to switch your choice?   

蒙蒂·霍尔问题是一个脑筋急转弯，以概率谜题的形式出现，名义上基于美国电视游戏节目《Let ' s Make a Deal》，并以其原主持人蒙蒂·霍尔的名字命名。这个问题最初是在史蒂夫·塞尔文（Steve Selvin）1975年写给美国统计学家的一封信中提出的。它作为读者克雷格·F的一个问题而闻名。1990年《游行》杂志的玛丽莲·沃斯·萨万特（Marilyn vos Savant）的“问玛丽莲”专栏中引用了惠特克的信：假设你正在参加一个游戏节目，你可以选择三扇门：一扇门后面是一辆汽车;其他门后面是山羊。你选择了一扇门，比如说1号门，而知道门后面有什么的主人会打开另一扇门，比如说3号门，里面有一只山羊。然后他对你说：“你想选2号门吗？“改变选择对你有利吗？

## 一月_2025_南部_加州_野火   
Starting on January 7, 2025, an ongoing series of catastrophic wildfires has affected the Los Angeles metropolitan area and surrounding regions. The fires, which include the Palisades Fire, Eaton Fire, Hurst Fire, and Sunset Fire were exacerbated by very low humidity, dry conditions, and hurricane-force Santa Ana winds that in some places exceeded 80–100 miles per hour (130–160 km/h). As of January 8, the wildfires have resulted in seven fatalities, destroyed or damaged more than 9,300 structures, and forced more than 179,000 people to evacuate.   

从2025年1月7日开始，一系列持续的灾难性野火影响了洛杉矶大都市区及周边地区。这些火灾包括帕利塞德斯火灾、伊顿火灾、赫斯特火灾和日落火灾，湿度极低、干燥的条件以及飓风强度的圣安娜风（在某些地方超过每小时80-100英里（130-160公里/小时））加剧。截至1月8日，野火已造成7人死亡，摧毁或损坏了9，300多座建筑，迫使超过179，000人撤离。

## Palisades_Fire_（2025）   
The Palisades Fire is a wildfire burning in the Santa Monica Mountains region of Los Angeles County in Southern California. As of January 9, 2025, at 2:40 p.m. PST, the fire had spread to almost 20,000 acres, devastating the Pacific Palisades neighborhood of Los Angeles and nearby Malibu. It is the first and largest of four major wildfires being driven by an extremely powerful Santa Ana wind event. On January 8, Wildfire Alliance statistics indicated that the fire is the most destructive fire in Los Angeles history, surpassing the Sayre Fire in Sylmar in 2008 which destroyed 604 structures. The cause of the fire is still under investigation.   

帕利塞德斯火灾是南加州洛杉矶县圣莫尼卡山脉地区燃烧的野火。截至太平洋标准时间2025年1月9日下午2：40，火势已蔓延至近20，000英亩，摧毁了洛杉矶太平洋帕利塞德社区和附近的马里布。这是由极其强大的圣安娜风事件引发的四场重大野火中的第一场也是最大的一场。1月8日，野火联盟的统计数据显示，这场火灾是洛杉矶历史上破坏性最大的火灾，超过了2008年西尔玛的塞尔火灾，该火灾摧毁了604座建筑物。火灾原因仍在调查中。
