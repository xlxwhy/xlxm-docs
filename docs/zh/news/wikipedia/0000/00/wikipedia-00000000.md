## 人类学   
Anthropology is the scientific study of humanity, concerned with human behavior, human biology, cultures, societies, and linguistics, in both the present and past, including archaic humans. Social anthropology studies patterns of behavior, while cultural anthropology studies cultural meaning, including norms and values. The term sociocultural anthropology is commonly used today. Linguistic anthropology studies how language influences social life. Biological or physical anthropology studies the biological development of humans.   

人类学是对人类的科学研究，关注现在和过去的人类行为、人类生物学、文化、社会和语言学，包括古代人类。社会人类学研究行为模式，而文化人类学研究文化意义，包括规范和价值观。社会文化人类学一词今天普遍使用。语言人类学研究语言如何影响社会生活。生物或体质人类学研究人类的生物发育。

## 拳击日   
Boxing Day is a holiday celebrated after Christmas Day, occurring on the second day of Christmastide. Boxing Day was once a day to donate gifts to those in need, but it has evolved to become a part of Christmas festivities, with many people choosing to shop for deals on Boxing Day. It originated in the United Kingdom and is celebrated in several Commonwealth nations. The attached bank holiday or public holiday may take place on 27 or 28 December if necessary to ensure it falls on a weekday. Boxing Day is also concurrent with the Christian festival Saint Stephen's Day.   

节礼日是圣诞节之后庆祝的节日，发生在圣诞节的第二天。节礼日曾经是向有需要的人捐赠礼物的日子，但它已经发展成为圣诞节庆祝活动的一部分，许多人选择在节礼日购物打折。它起源于英国，并在几个英联邦国家庆祝。如果有必要，附加的银行假日或公众假日可能会在12月27日或28日举行，以确保其在工作日。节礼日也与基督教节日圣斯蒂芬节同时发生。

## 圣诞   
Christmas is an annual festival commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ, observed primarily on December 25 as a religious and cultural celebration among billions of people around the world. A liturgical feast central to Christianity, preparation for Christmas begins on the First Sunday of Advent and it is followed by Christmastide, which historically in the West lasts twelve days and culminates on Twelfth Night. Christmas Day is a public holiday in many countries, is observed religiously by a majority of Christians, as well as celebrated culturally by many non-Christians, and forms an integral part of the annual holiday season.   

圣诞节是纪念耶稣基督诞生的一年一度的节日，主要在12月25日庆祝，作为世界各地数十亿人的宗教和文化庆祝活动。圣诞节是基督教的核心礼拜盛宴，圣诞节的准备工作从降临节的第一个星期日开始，随后是圣诞节，历史上，在西方，圣诞节持续十二天，在第十二夜达到高潮。圣诞节在许多国家都是公共假日，大多数基督徒都以宗教方式庆祝，许多非基督徒也在文化上庆祝，并且是一年一度假期不可或缺的一部分。
