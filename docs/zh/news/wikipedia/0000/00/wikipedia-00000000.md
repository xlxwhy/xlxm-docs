## 曼莫汉·辛格   
Manmohan Singh was an Indian politician, economist, academic, and bureaucrat, who served as the 13th prime minister of India from 2004 to 2014. He was the fourth longest-serving prime minister after Jawaharlal Nehru, Indira Gandhi and Narendra Modi. A member of the Indian National Congress, Singh was the first Sikh prime minister of India. He was also the first prime minister since Jawaharlal Nehru to be re-elected after completing a full five-year term.

## 拳击日   
Boxing Day is a holiday celebrated after Christmas Day, occurring on the second day of Christmastide. Boxing Day was once a day to donate gifts to those in need, but it has evolved to become a part of Christmas festivities, with many people choosing to shop for deals on Boxing Day. It originated in the United Kingdom and is celebrated in several Commonwealth nations. The attached bank holiday or public holiday may take place on 27 or 28 December if necessary to ensure it falls on a weekday. Boxing Day is also concurrent with the Christian festival Saint Stephen's Day.   

节礼日是圣诞节之后庆祝的节日，发生在圣诞节的第二天。节礼日曾经是向有需要的人捐赠礼物的日子，但它已经发展成为圣诞节庆祝活动的一部分，许多人选择在节礼日购物打折。它起源于英国，并在几个英联邦国家庆祝。如果有必要，附加的银行假日或公众假日可能会在12月27日或28日举行，以确保其在工作日。节礼日也与基督教节日圣斯蒂芬节同时发生。
