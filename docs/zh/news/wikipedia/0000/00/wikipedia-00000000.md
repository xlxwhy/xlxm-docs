## 2025年堪察加半岛地震   
On 30 July 2025, at 11:24:50 PETT, an Mw 8.8 megathrust earthquake struck off the eastern coast of the Kamchatka Peninsula in Russia's far east, 119 km (74 mi) east-southeast of the coastal city of Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky. It was the most powerful earthquake recorded since the 2011 Tōhoku earthquake, and is tied with the 1906 Ecuador–Colombia and the 2010 Chile earthquakes as the sixth-strongest earthquake ever recorded by seismometers. The earthquake and subsequent Pacific-wide tsunami caused moderate damage and multiple injuries in Kamchatka Krai and Sakhalin Oblast.   

2025年7月30日，太平洋时间11：24：50，俄罗斯远东堪察加半岛东海岸发生里氏8.8级地震，距离沿海城市堪察加彼得罗巴甫洛夫斯克东南偏东119公里。这是自2011年东北地震以来记录到的最强烈的地震，与1906年厄瓜多尔-哥伦比亚地震和2010年智利地震并列为地震仪记录到的第六强地震。地震和随后的太平洋海啸在堪察加边疆区和萨哈林州造成中度破坏和多人受伤。

## 2004年_印度_海洋_地震_和_海啸   
On 26 December 2004, at 07:58:53 local time (UTC+7), a major earthquake with a magnitude of 9.2–9.3 Mw struck with an epicentre off the west coast of Aceh in northern Sumatra, Indonesia. The undersea megathrust earthquake, known by the scientific community as the Sumatra–Andaman earthquake, was caused by a rupture along the fault between the Burma plate and the Indian plate, and reached a Mercalli intensity of IX in some areas.   

2004年12月26日当地时间07：58：53（UTC+7），震中位于印度尼西亚苏门答腊北部亚齐西海岸附近，发生震级为9.2-9.3 MW的大地震。这次海底大逆冲地震被科学界称为苏门答腊-安达曼地震，是由缅甸板块和印度板块之间断层的破裂引起的，部分地区的麦卡利强度达到九级。

## 堪察加半岛   
The Kamchatka Peninsula is a 1,250-kilometre-long (777 mi) peninsula in the Russian Far East, with an area of about 270,000 km2 (100,000 sq mi). The Sea of Okhotsk bounds the peninsula's western coastline, and immediately offshore of the peninsula and the Okhotsk runs the 9,600-metre-deep (31,496 ft) Kuril–Kamchatka Trench. Its eastern coastline is made up of the Bering Sea, part of the Pacific Ocean.   

堪察加半岛是俄罗斯远东地区一个长1，250公里（777英里）的半岛，面积约270，000平方公里（100，000平方英里）。鄂霍次克海接壤半岛西部海岸线，紧邻半岛近海，鄂霍次克海贯穿9，600米深（31，496英尺）的千岛-堪察加海沟。其东部海岸线由太平洋的一部分白令海组成。

## 2011年_东北_地震_海啸   
On 11 March 2011, at 14:46:24 JST, a Mw 9.0–9.1 undersea megathrust earthquake occurred in the Pacific Ocean, 72 km (45 mi) east of the Oshika Peninsula of the Tōhoku region. It lasted approximately six minutes and caused a tsunami. It is sometimes known in Japan as the "Great East Japan Earthquake" , among other names. The disaster is often referred to by its numerical date, 3.11.   

2011年3月11日，日本夏令时间14：46：24，东北地区押贺半岛以东72公里（45英里）的太平洋发生了9.0-9.1级海底大逆冲地震。持续了大约六分钟并引发了海啸。它在日本有时被称为“东日本大地震”等名称。这场灾难通常用其数字日期3.11来指代。
