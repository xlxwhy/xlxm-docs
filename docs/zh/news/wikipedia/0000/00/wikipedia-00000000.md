## 曼莫汉·辛格   
Manmohan Singh was an Indian politician, economist, academic, and bureaucrat, who served as the 13th prime minister of India from 2004 to 2014. ## 拳击日   
Boxing Day is a holiday celebrated after Christmas Day, occurring on the second day of Christmastide. Boxing Day was once a day to donate gifts to those in need, but it has evolved to become a part of Christmas festivities, with many people choosing to shop for deals on Boxing Day. It originated in the United Kingdom and is celebrated in several Commonwealth nations. The attached bank holiday or public holiday may take place on 27 or 28 December if necessary to ensure it falls on a weekday. Boxing Day is also concurrent with the Christian festival Saint Stephen's Day.   

节礼日是圣诞节之后庆祝的节日，发生在圣诞节的第二天。节礼日曾经是向有需要的人捐赠礼物的日子，但它已经发展成为圣诞节庆祝活动的一部分，许多人选择在节礼日购物打折。它起源于英国，并在几个英联邦国家庆祝。如果有必要，附加的银行假日或公众假日可能会在12月27日或28日举行，以确保其在工作日。节礼日也与基督教节日圣斯蒂芬节同时发生。

## 2004年_印度_海洋_地震_和_海啸   
On 26 December 2004, at 07:58:53 local time (UTC+7), a major earthquake with a magnitude of 9.2–9.3 Mw struck with an epicentre off the west coast of Aceh in northern Sumatra, Indonesia. The undersea megathrust earthquake, known by the scientific community as the Sumatra–Andaman earthquake, was caused by a rupture along the fault between the Burma plate and the Indian plate, and reached a Mercalli intensity of IX in some areas.   

2004年12月26日当地时间07：58：53（UTC+7），震中位于印度尼西亚苏门答腊北部亚齐西海岸附近，发生震级为9.2-9.3 MW的大地震。这次海底大逆冲地震被科学界称为苏门答腊-安达曼地震，是由缅甸板块和印度板块之间断层的破裂引起的，部分地区的麦卡利强度达到九级。

## 宽扎节   
Kwanzaa is an annual celebration of African-American culture from December 26 to January 1, culminating in a communal feast called Karamu, usually on the sixth day. It was created by activist Maulana Karenga, based on African harvest festival traditions from various parts of West, East, as well as Southeast Africa. Kwanzaa was first celebrated in 1966. Twenty-first-century estimates place the number of Americans who celebrate Kwanzaa between 500,000 and 2,000,000.   

宽扎节是一年一度的非裔美国人文化庆祝活动，活动时间为12月26日至1月1日，通常在第六天举行名为Karamu的公共盛宴。它由活动家Maulana Karenga创建，基于西非、东非和东南非洲各地的非洲丰收节传统。宽扎节于1966年首次庆祝。据21世纪估计，庆祝宽扎节的美国人人数在50万至2，000，000人之间。

Carry-On is a 2024 American action thriller film directed by Jaume Collet-Serra and written by T.J. Fixman. The film stars Taron Egerton, Sofia Carson, Danielle Deadwyler and Jason Bateman. Its plot follows a young TSA officer who's blackmailed into letting a nerve agent onboard a flight during Christmas Eve.   

Carry-On是一部2024年的美国动作惊悚电影，由Jaume Collet-Serra执导，TJ Fixman编剧。这部电影由塔隆·埃格顿、索菲亚·卡森、丹妮尔·德怀勒和杰森·贝特曼主演。故事情节讲述了一名年轻的运输安全管理局官员在圣诞节前夕被勒索让神经毒剂登上航班的故事。
