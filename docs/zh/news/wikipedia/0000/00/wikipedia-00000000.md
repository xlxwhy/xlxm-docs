## 卡洛_Acutis   
Carlo Acutis was a British-born Italian student celebrated for his devotion to the Eucharist and his use of digital media to promote Catholic devotion. Born in London and raised in Milan, he created a website documenting Eucharistic miracles and Marian apparitions. Diagnosed with leukemia, he died at age fifteen. ## 九月_2025_月_日食   
A total lunar eclipse occurred at the Moon's ascending node of orbit on Sunday, September 7, 2025, with an umbral magnitude of 1.3638. A lunar eclipse occurs when the Moon moves into the Earth's shadow, causing the Moon to be darkened. A total lunar eclipse occurs when the Moon's near side entirely passes into the Earth's umbral shadow. Unlike a solar eclipse, which can only be viewed from a relatively small area of the world, a lunar eclipse may be viewed from anywhere on the night side of Earth. A total lunar eclipse can last up to nearly two hours, while a total solar eclipse lasts only a few minutes at any given place, because the Moon's shadow is smaller. Occurring about 2.6 days before perigee, the Moon's apparent diameter was larger.   

2025年9月7日星期日，月球轨道上升节发生月全食，本影星等为1.3638。当月球移动到地球的阴影中时，就会发生月食，导致月球变暗。当月球的近侧完全进入地球的本影时，就会发生月全食。与只能从世界上相对较小的区域观看日食不同，月食可以从地球夜侧的任何地方观看。月全食可以持续近两个小时，而日全食在任何特定地点只持续几分钟，因为月球的阴影较小。月球的视直径在近地点前2.6天左右较大。

## 月食   
A lunar eclipse is an astronomical event that occurs when the Moon moves into the Earth's shadow, causing the Moon to be darkened. Such an alignment occurs during an eclipse season, approximately every six months, during the full moon phase, when the Moon's orbital plane is closest to the plane of the Earth's orbit. This can occur only when the Sun, Earth, and Moon are exactly or very closely aligned with Earth between the other two, which can happen only on the night of a full moon when the Moon is near either lunar node. The type and length of a lunar eclipse depend on the Moon's proximity to the lunar node.   

月食是一种天文现象，当月球移动到地球的阴影中时，导致月球变暗。这种对齐发生在日食季节，大约每六个月一次，即满月阶段，此时月球的轨道平面最接近地球的轨道平面。只有当太阳、地球和月球在另外两者之间与地球完全或非常紧密地对齐时，这种情况才会发生，而这种情况只会发生在满月之夜，此时月球靠近任何一个月球节点。月食的类型和长度取决于月球与月球节点的接近程度。
